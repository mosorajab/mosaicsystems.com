01 / About Profile

I build machine-learning systems the way I appreciate a well-made object: with attention to mechanism, material, and the quiet details most people never notice.

I'm a Machine Learning Engineer at Derivco, where I take models from research into dependable production. My background is in data science; I completed an honours degree cum laude, built on an undergraduate foundation in application development, so I'm as comfortable shipping the interface as I am training the model behind it.

Alongside that, I run Mosaic Systems, a technology company delivering real, tech-forward solutions to its clients, across AI, data, automation, home labs, and hardware. It's where I take ideas the whole way: practical intelligence, built deliberately, for real use.

The thread through all of it is craft. I'm drawn to things that are engineered to last and rewarding to use, which is probably why, away from the keyboard, I'm usually found with a fountain pen, a mechanical watch, or a good book in reach.
